Our Philosophy

Back to what our grandmothers already knew.

Nirmanya was founded on a simple observation: the ingredients Sri Lankan households have trusted for generations were being replaced on store shelves by long lists of chemicals. We bring those ingredients back to the front of the label.

We work in small batches, with local growers, so every bar of soap and every bottle of oil can be traced back to where it grew.
