Where it began

A return to what our grandmothers already knew.

Nirmanya was founded on a simple observation: the ingredients Sri Lankan households have trusted for generations — turmeric, coconut, curry leaf, gotukola — were being replaced on store shelves by long lists of chemicals we couldn't pronounce.

Nirmanya brings those ingredients back to the front of the label, formulated for modern routines but sourced the way they always should have been. We work in small batches, with local growers, so every bar of soap and every bottle of oil can be traced back to where it grew.

From Field to Bottle

How a Nirmanya product is made

1

Sourced from local growers

Turmeric, neem, coconut, curry leaf and gotukola are sourced directly from small Sri Lankan farms we work with personally.

2

Cold-processed in small batches

Low-heat methods preserve the natural actives in every ingredient — no shortcuts, no fillers, no artificial preservatives.

3

Hand-finished and packaged

Each batch is finished and packed by hand in recyclable, minimal packaging. No mass production — ever.

4

Delivered islandwide

Ordered through our website or social pages, and delivered with Cash-on-Delivery available across Sri Lanka.
