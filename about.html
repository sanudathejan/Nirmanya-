Our Story

Your comfort, close to the earth, is our mission.

Where it began

A return to what our grandmothers already knew.

Nirmanya was founded on a simple observation: the ingredients Sri Lankan households have trusted for generations — turmeric, coconut, curry leaf, gotukola — were being replaced on store shelves by long lists of chemicals we couldn't pronounce. Nirmanya brings those ingredients back to the front of the label, formulated for modern routines but sourced the way they always should have been.

We work in small batches, with local growers, so every bar of soap and every bottle of oil can be traced back to where it grew.

From Field to Bottle

How a Nirmanya product is made

Step 01

Sourced from local growers

Turmeric, neem, coconut, curry leaf and gotukola are sourced directly from small Sri Lankan farms.

Step 02

Cold-processed in small batches

Low-heat methods preserve the natural actives in every ingredient — no shortcuts, no fillers.

Step 03

Hand-finished and packaged

Each batch is finished and packed by hand in recyclable, minimal packaging.

Step 04

Delivered islandwide

Ordered through our website or social pages, and delivered with Cash-on-Delivery available.
